About

For more than three decades, I've worked in the world of design, marketing, and advertising. As a creative director, I foster a culture creative openness while also bringing strategic direction to my teams.

While I grew up on the beaches of California, my career has taken me across the country to work with some of the biggest brands in the industry.

My experience in print, television, and the digital sphere has been guided by my desire to build relationships between brands and their consumers.

I'm constantly stretching my limits and exploring new ways of connecting with consumers by creating unique ideas to increase their brand awareness.
